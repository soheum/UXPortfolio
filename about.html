I believe interaction design is all about relationships and among different complex relationships and as an Interaction Designer, I want to establish an stable connection between users and technology. While using technology as a means, not an outcome, what I design should contain technological affordances and deliver value for users. While both working in large and small-scale in a design consultancy and in a startup, I have experience in UX/UI design, strategic design, service design and designing with data. Besides design, I love exploring spaces that gives a warm ambiance and this lead me to a passion in interior design and photography.
