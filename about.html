Currently studying Interaction Design in Umeå, Sweden, I try to achieve both technological advancement and social values. With a background of UX design in BA, I worked in a UX consultancy in Korea, making customer journey maps and constructing both online and offline user exepriences. As a UX/UI Designer, I also worked in a Finnish startup and continued working remotely to finish launching the service. I love to give an interactive touch to digital products. After all, it is about making our experiences enjoyable!
Besides design, I love to take "the richness of going slow" approach with taking film photography.
