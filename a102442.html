Used Tools
42 is a pregnancy toolkit that is used by not only pregnant women, but also their husbands. This toolkit enhances communication and understanding between the wife and the husband by having cards that contain information about pregnancy. Also by coloring each card, it enhances pregnant women’s prenatal education as it completes the whole photo in the end of the usage.

Since none of us in our team had experience with pregnancy, we utilized different user research methods in order to understand the main user of our service. Furthermore, we had to think of a completely new solution either than traditional books or applications.
We conducted an interview with wives and husbands who have experience in pregnancy.
However, we discovered couples who experienced pregnancy more than 10 years ago do not illustrate the same experience compared to the present, which led us to interview again, only with couples who experienced pregnancy less than 5 years from now.
