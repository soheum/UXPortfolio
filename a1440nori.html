Nori Nuri is an interactive play-education platform for 3 to 5 year old children. Instead of being exposed to harmful contents through smart devices, children can interact with their favorite characters who are actually teachers giving out 30 minute long classes.

Children in Korea are being exposed to harmful media through smart devices. Their brain development is hindered, and blocking services are inefficient. Our solution tackles both problems, especially regarding smart devices now have become an irresistable childcare tool.
