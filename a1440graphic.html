Graphic Design
Category
Duration

Illustrator
Used Tools
This calendar series established a system through Buckminster Fuller’s 3d geodesic dome. Each month of the calendar analyzes how this 3d hemisphere structure developed by an American architect Fuller, developed from a 2D cubotahedron shape to a 3d dome.

Fuller’s geodesic dome goes through various stages: a cuboctahedron, Jitterburg Transformation, geodesic dome, tensegrity structure. This series illustrates how each stage contributes to the making of a geodesic dome, and how each of these parts affect in creating a visual system.
