Talk, Think, Play!
Nori Nuri


an interactive play-education platform for 3 to 5 year old children, where children interact with their favorite characters who are actually teachers giving out 30-min long classes

TEAM

Doyeon Gu, Yoonjin Choi and me

MY ROLE

User Research, UX / UI Design, User Testing, Branding & Graphics

AWARDS

Final Video

Design Process: Discover

Smartphone now has become an inevitable tool for babysitting.

We conducted an interview and an online survey with mothers and fathers of 3 to 6 year old children in online communities. This gave our team insights on children’s smartphone usage and their habits.

Mother of a 3 year old child

“My son now expects me to give my smartphone when we go to a restaurant.”

Father of a 4 year old child

“Blocking services are useless anyways, so I always have to see the smart device together.”

Mother of a 4 year old child

“Smartphones are the most effective solution for babysitting, both time wise and money wise.”

Design Process: Define

How might we reduce the side effects of children’s inevitable media usage?

If children are going to use smartphones anyway, why not just provide a method to use them wisely? Smartphone usage is already prevalent, and the blocking programs are not even working either.

User Testing

Two main user tests

There were in total of two user tests with a 5 year old and a 4 year old child. The difference of showing Nori in beforehand resulted in different level of interest, which also resulted in different class duration. This led us to include an introductory class that children can take with their parents, being introduced to new characters as if they are their new friend.

First user test - didn't know Nori beforehand

01 started test right away

the child didn't had any information about the service, or the class he was going to take

02 20 minutes of class

read stories with Nori and summarized the story afterhand

Second user test - exposed to Nori beforehand

01 knew who Nori was

the child was told by her mom that she is going to call Nori and play with him

02 40 minutes of class

the child even searched for where Nori was when the class was stopped for a while due to low wifi connection

"Beginner's Curriculum" added after user testing

Instead of children meeting Nori and Nuri straight away, we included a class where they can take with their parents. Here, they will be introduced to Nori and Nuri as if they were meeting their new friend.
