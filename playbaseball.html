Can we measure how talented an umpire is?

Instead of baseball statistics measured on players, can an umpire's statistics be evaluated as well? How correct is an umpire deciding which pitch is a strike or a ball?
With Korean Baseball League's statistics, we visualized how accurate an umpire can be in his role through an interactive data visualization website.
